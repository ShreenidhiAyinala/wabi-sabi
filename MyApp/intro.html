Welcome to Wabi-Sabi!

Story

The martial warrior is a protector of nature. He protects the forest from different types of pollution. Pollution is the introduction of contaminants into the natural environment that cause adverse change.

Different Types of Pollution Include:
  • Air Pollution
  • Water Pollution
  • Land Pollution

    • Effects of Pollution Include:
  • Respiratory Infections
  • Heart Disease
  • Lung Cancer

    • On the other hand, Kenji is an evil sorcerer who releases harmful pollutants each minute he is alive.

    Your MISSION, should you choose to accept it, is to defeat Kenji and save the forest from being polluted.
